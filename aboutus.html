About Us

Sunbeam was founded in 1972 by Dr.Amrit lal 'Ishrat' Madhok and Mrs. Deesh 'Ishrat' Madhok, who felt the need of a private educational
institution to cater to the holistic educational needs of the students fraternity in Varanasi and thus was born Sunbeam. Sunbeam started its
momentous journey in a small rented apartment. Armed with a vision plus boundless energy and gaining credibility by the day, giant strides were
made and the young institution grew rapidly in enrolment, quality and community recognition and soon outgrew its place of birth and it became
obvious that a new location was needed.

innumerable CBSE board toppers, national and international players of repute and stars who are shining bright in the cultural horizon.

As a group, Sunbeam truly epitomizes the fact that success is a journey and not a destination.
