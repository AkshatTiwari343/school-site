FAREWELL

Every year we give a farewell party to all the class 12th students which is mostly organised by the students of class
11th and funded by the school.It is a very huge event with a dance shows and other entertainment programs. After all the
programs take place there is refreshments and students get to spend time with there friends . After this meetup between the
students our chairman sir addresses us. After this everyone enjoys the dinner and henceforth,the event is over.
The dresscode is normally formal coat-suit for boys and saries for girls.
The event takes place in the evening.
